### Oracle Input
$$\frac{\ket{0}+\ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}}$$
### Constant & Balanced Result
$$\ket{\psi_0}=\frac{\ket{0}+\ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}},\ket{\psi_1}\frac{\ket{0}-\ket{1}}{\sqrt{2}}$$
### Hadamard Result
$$H\ket{\psi_0}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(H\ket{0}+H\ket{1})$$
$$=\frac{1}{2}(\ket{0}+\ket{1}+\ket{0}-\ket{1})=\ket{0}$$
$$H\ket{\psi_1}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(H\ket{0}-H\ket{1})$$
$$=\frac{1}{2}(\ket{0}+\ket{1}-\ket{0}+\ket{1})=\ket{1}$$
